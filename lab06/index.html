Website Usability Evaluation: National Park Service

1. URL of the Website

https://www.nps.gov/

2. Name of the Website

National Park Service

3. Target Audience

The target audience includes outdoor enthusiasts, families planning trips, educators, and anyone interested in national parks and conservation.

4. Site Organization

The site is organized with a clear top navigation bar that includes links to Parks, Learn About Parks, and Plan Your Visit, making it easy for users to find information.

5. CRAP Design Principle

The site effectively uses the Alignment principle by ensuring that text and images are neatly aligned, enhancing readability and aesthetic appeal.

6. Audit Score

The accessibility checker score was 76/100.

7. Effectiveness

The site supports users in completing tasks accurately, with a robust search function and informative content that helps users find park details and resources.

8. Efficiency

Users can perform tasks quickly due to the logical layout and the presence of a prominent search feature that streamlines navigation.

9. Engagement

The site is visually appealing and provides engaging content, including stunning imagery of parks, making it pleasant to use and appropriate for its educational purpose.

10. Recommendation for Improvement

To improve accessibility, consider adding ARIA labels to interactive elements and enhancing keyboard navigation options for users with disabilities.
